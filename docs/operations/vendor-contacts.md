## ðŸš¨ Emergency Response Protocol

### When to Contact Vendors

**Immediate Contact (Within 15 minutes)**

- Complete service outage
- Data loss or corruption
- Security breach
- Payment processing failures

**Urgent Contact (Within 1 hour)**

- Performance degradation
- Partial service outage
- Authentication failures
- Critical feature failures

**Standard Contact (Within 4 hours)**

- Non-critical bugs
- Performance issues
- Configuration problems
- General questions

### Contact Process

1. **Gather Information**
   - Account IDs, project IDs
   - Error messages and logs
   - Timeline of events
   - Impact assessment

2. **Choose Contact Method**
   - Use fastest available channel
   - Phone for critical issues
   - Chat for urgent issues
   - Email for standard issues

3. **Provide Context**
   - Business impact
   - Timeline requirements
   - Previous troubleshooting steps
   - Expected resolution time

4. **Document Everything**
   - Ticket numbers
   - Contact names
   - Resolution steps
   - Follow-up requirements

### Escalation Triggers

**Immediate Escalation**

- No response within SLA time
- Issue not resolved within 2x SLA time
- Customer impact increasing
- Revenue impact > $1000/hour

**Escalation Contacts**

- **Internal**: CTO, VP Engineering
- **Vendor**: Account Manager, Enterprise Support
- **Executive**: CEO (for critical outages)
